Fashion Photography

With the expansion of the internet, fashion photographers are increasingly in demand. In this course you'll learn the fundamental skills involved in taking original fashion images for a variety of commissioners, from magazines to designers. You will focus on the practical photography techniques and develop your photographic eye, in order to deliver fashion photos to a professional standard.

The course includes daily feedback critique sessions, practical tips about cameras and studio lighting, a retouching workshop, discussions about presenting work (websites and iPad portfolios), practical mood-board development, and industry level guest speakers for Q&A sessions.Lastly, working as part of a creative team, you will produce and execute a day-long fashion shoot using a professional industry team of models, fashion stylists, make-up artists and hair stylists, adding valuable content to your fashion photography portfolio.

Who should take this course?

  • Students with an interest in fashion styling and photography
  • Photographers, stylists and art directors
