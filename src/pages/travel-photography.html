Travel Photography

On this travel photography course you will explore and capture with originality some of the infinite photographic possibilities in one of the world's top tourist attractions, London. By the end of the course you will have a thorough understanding of creative composition, colour films and camera controls for all situations including low light interiors and night time photography. The aim is to apply these skills to all photographic situations to create a travel story with style and confidence that is both original and commercial. Photoshop, scanning, digital manipulation and producing files ready for publication is also covered.

In this course you will learn about the history of documentary photography and contemporary documentary practice, explored through the work of influential photographers. By comparing a diverse range of photographic approaches and by becoming familiar with photo shooting and editing techniques you will develop your own storytelling skills to communicate your project ideas. Throughout the course you will work on assignments and gain invaluable feedback from an award-winning photographer.

Course outcomes

By the end of this course you will have:

  • Use photo shooting techniques
  • Use DSLR settings effectively
  • Know how to develop a personal project
  • Edit your own photographs
